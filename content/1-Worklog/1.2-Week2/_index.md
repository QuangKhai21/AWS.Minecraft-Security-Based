### Week 2 Objectives

* Practice AWS cost management using AWS Budgets and usage alerts.
* Understand the differences between Cost Budget, Usage Budget, Reserved Instances, and Savings Plans.
* Build a routine for monitoring and cleaning up unused AWS resources.
